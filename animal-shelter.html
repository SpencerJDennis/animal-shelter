My Animal Shelter

Daisy

This is daisy! She is a 4 year old white and black Border Collie. Daisy is very energetic, but kind dog. Daisy loves to go on walks, and socialize with other dogs at our local dog park. She is not a fussy eater, and does not beg. She works well with other pets and has no history of biting. Daisy is potty trained and very intelligent.

Pudgey

This is Pudgey! He is a 10 year old orange tabby cat. Pudgey is a very independent, but loving cat. He loves to bask in the sun in or outdoors. He is not very social with other animals, but can be conditioned to other pets. He is litter boxed trained, and loves treats. Pudgey needs a loving and peacful home to move into.

Torrent

This is Torrent! He is a 5 year old snapping tutle. Torrent is a very independent animal, in need of a knowledgeable owner well versed in the care of tutles. He will need his own terrarium, and is on a specialized diet. Torrent is worth it though! He does not mind being handled or held, though watch your fingers around his bite.
